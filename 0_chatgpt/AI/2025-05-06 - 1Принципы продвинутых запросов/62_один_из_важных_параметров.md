# TLDR: The Practical Significance Framework - From Engineering Intuition to Cognitive Architecture

## Introduction: Beyond Statistical Significance

In traditional AI engineering, we often focus on statistical significance as the primary measure of value. However, this perspective misses a crucial distinction between mere progress and meaningful advancement[^1]. When evaluating knowledge or methods for practical application, the key differentiator lies in whether they enable progression along a measurable target parameter from minimal to maximum possible levels [^2].

## The Four Essential Criteria

Building upon foundational principles, I propose four additional criteria that define truly practical significance:

### 1. Energy Efficiency of Knowledge
Practical knowledge must be computationally efficient compared to alternatives. A solution requiring millions of iterations and supercomputers loses to one that achieves the same results through five heuristics embedded in human cognition[^2].

> **Criterion**: "Does this knowledge outperform others on energy-informational efficiency?"

### 2. Noise Resistance
Knowledge should survive under conditions of incomplete, erroneous, or partially distorted information. This resilience reflects real-world conditions where data is often fragmented rather than perfect [^1].

> **Criterion**: "Does the knowledge survive when 40% of input parameters are lost?"

### 3. Modularity 
Practical significance requires that knowledge can be integrated as a module into other systems without requiring full system overhauls[^2]. It should function independently, creating improvement through insertion rather than complete replacement.

> **Criterion**: "Does this knowledge work in foreign contexts without complete system transfer?"

### 4. Evolutionary Load
The most valuable practical insights must enhance the evolutionary capacity of the systems they're integrated into, whether that's organisms, intelligence structures, or strategic frameworks[^1].

> **Criterion**: "Does it increase systemic complexity without creating chaos?"

## From Engineering Perspective to Cognitive Architecture

This framework reveals a fundamental shift from traditional engineering thinking to cognitive architecture.
