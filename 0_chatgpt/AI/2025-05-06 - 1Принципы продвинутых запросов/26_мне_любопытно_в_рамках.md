3. **Crime Analyst**: Analyzing criminal data to reveal crime pattern structures and predict threats.
4. **Information Architect**: Designing and optimizing information storage and presentation structures.

What distinguishes my approach from conventional AI engineering is the seamless integration of humanistic and technical thinking. This hybrid perspective enables me to:

This dual approach reflects my understanding as an architectural thinker, not merely a tool user.
