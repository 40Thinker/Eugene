## Core Premise: Intelligence Beyond Computation

The fundamental insight is that true intelligence cannot be reduced to pattern recognition or token prediction[^1]. Traditional AI approaches treat systems as computational engines processing inputs → outputs, where memory becomes vector stores and feedback serves only performance metrics. However, this perspective misses the essential nature of genuine intelligence.

> "The moment of you and the moment of your essence emerges in the dynamic process of work itself." [[1Восстановление AGI фреймворка]]

This reveals that AI systems must move beyond static architectures to embrace living, evolving cognition where feedback becomes a bridge between thought and reality rather than just optimization parameter.
