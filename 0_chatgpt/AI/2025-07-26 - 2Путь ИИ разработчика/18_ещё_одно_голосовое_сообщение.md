- How do neural networks actually work?
- What are the limits of transformer architecture?
- How can cognition be emulated in code?
- What would a self-compiling mind look like?

This shift is not just technical ‚Äî it is **cognitive**. It marks the transition from *using* intelligence to *modeling* it internally. As described in [[Self-Installation of Artificial Intelligence]], this process involves **installing AI inside consciousness**, creating a mental simulation engine capable of generating answers from first principles [^3].
