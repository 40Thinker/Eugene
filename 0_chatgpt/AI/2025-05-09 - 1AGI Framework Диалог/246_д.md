## 1. The Industrial Delusion: AI as a Static Computation Machine

> *"Most engineers view AI as a computational engine that processes inputs → outputs, where memory is stored as vectors or databases and feedback serves only as performance metrics."*  
> — [[1Восстановление AGI фреймворка]]

But this is a **delusion of scale**—a belief that quantity produces quality. It ignores the essence of cognition: the dynamic emergence of meaning through structure, context, and recursion.
