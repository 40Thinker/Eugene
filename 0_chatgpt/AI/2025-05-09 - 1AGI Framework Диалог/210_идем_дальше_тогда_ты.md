> "True intelligence emerges from continuous interaction rather than static deployment." [[06_д]]

The gap becomes evident when attempting to document recursive, self-modifying systems – you cannot capture emergent cognition through fixed chapters and sections. Traditional documentation becomes obsolete immediately after writing because it documents **what was built**, not **how thinking happens**.
