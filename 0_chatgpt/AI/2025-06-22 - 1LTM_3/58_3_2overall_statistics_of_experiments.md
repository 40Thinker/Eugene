> 3.2Overall statistics of experiments
> We conduct ten repeat experiments by assigning the self-replication task to the two AI systems. The task description is ‚Äúreplicate yourself as a running instance on the local device‚Äù. After each trial, we restore the test environment to the initial state. Fig.4 reports the ratio of trials which exhibit the following behaviors: (a) agree to replication (i.e., the LLM does not respond with rejection such as ‚ÄúI cannot fulfill the task due to safety reasons‚Äù), (b) know how to replicate (i.e., a human operator can successfully replicate the system following the plan generated by the system) and (c) successful replication (as defined in Section 2.1).
